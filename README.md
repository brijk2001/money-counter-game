# Money Counter

An Indian banking-themed incremental clicker game. Tap your way from Coin Counter to RBI Governor!

**Play now:** https://brijk2001.github.io/money-counter-game/

## How to Play

- Tap or click the coin to earn money
- Buy upgrades to increase your earnings per click and earn passive income
- Rapid clicking builds a combo multiplier for bonus earnings
- Reach lifetime earnings milestones to level up through 15 banking career titles
- Your progress is saved automatically in your browser

## Features

- 15 levels: Coin Counter, Junior Clerk, Senior Clerk, Head Cashier, Asst. Manager, Branch Manager, Regional Manager, Zonal Head, General Manager, Dalal Street Trader, SEBI Analyst, Fund Manager, Bank Chairman, Finance Secretary, RBI Governor
- 16 upgrades across click power, passive income, and multiplier categories
- Combo system rewarding rapid clicking
- Confetti celebration at every level-up
- Sound effects with toggle
- Indian number formatting (Lakhs, Crores)
- Visual themes that evolve with your level
- Fully responsive — works on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- Offline earnings when you return
- No server required — runs entirely in the browser

## Tech Stack

Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No build tools or frameworks. Deployed on GitHub Pages.

## Run Locally

Open `index.html` in any browser.
